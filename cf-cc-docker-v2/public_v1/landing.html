Content Clarifier

This app serves as a demo for the IBM AbilityLab™ Content Clarifier API, a Cognitive Computing effort to perform content simplification, summarization, and enhancement.


The IBM AbilityLab™ Content Clarifier is a callable API that builds upon IBM Watson deep learning APIs to include a service that dynamically simplifies, summarizes or enhances content to increase comprehension. In the demo, there are three modes of output:

Simplified: Lexical and grammatical manipulation is performed to improve comprehension. Non-literal expressions such as idioms and colloquialisms are also replaced with their simple English counterparts. Simplified mode also supports enabling "enhanced content", which returns simple English definitions, as well as additional information about identified topics.

Condensed: Content is summarized for quick reading.

Ultra-Mode: A second pass of simplification is performed on summarized content.
